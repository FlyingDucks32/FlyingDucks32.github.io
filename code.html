Welcome to Worldwide Musical Tutors

About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free musical lessons to children around the world.

Our volunteer tutors are passionate about music and committed to helping children develop their musical skills and talents.

Our Tutors

We have a team of experienced music teachers and performers who are eager to share their knowledge and skills with young musicians.

Our tutors come from a variety of musical backgrounds and specialties, including classical, jazz, rock, and more.
